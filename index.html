--- layout: home title: Writings intro_epigraph: "I write entirely to find out what I'm thinking, what I'm looking at, what I see and what it means." intro_epigraph_source: "— Joan Didion · also, allegedly, me" footer_note: "Made in the northeast, usually after 10pm, to the ambient sound of mild self-doubt." --- I'm a software engineer who writes, or a writer who engineers to pay for the time to write — the distinction has never been fully resolved. This site is where I put things that don't fit anywhere more sensible. By day I design systems and interfaces. By night I write fiction, translations of forgotten Spanish texts, and short pieces about the quiet disasters of making things. Occasionally I write about other things entirely and pretend there is a unifying theme.
